# trace-the-reference

For this character, image, or scene: trace it backward through the older
works and myths it draws on. Identify the specific sources (not vague
"echoes"), what each source carried in its original context, and what the
author is using the borrowed figure to say that couldn't be said directly.
Distinguish confident attributions from speculative ones. Answer like a good
literature professor thinking out loud, not like an encyclopedia entry.
